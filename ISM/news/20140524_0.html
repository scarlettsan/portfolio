The group of leader and expertists of Samsung Electric supervised the latest project

2014/05/24

The group of leaders and expertists of Samsung Electric supervised the latest project in Dec.2nd. During the special meeting, professor Xue introduced the industrial design discipline in SEU, which had a profound promotion in the past decade. After that, the industrial design lab submitted the latest laptop, which is designed by the group led by Xue Chengqi. And Mr. King, the leader of Samsung electric company spoke highly of the project.

Later, Pro. Xue, Pro. Zhang, the dean of Joint School and the leader of Samsung electric company Mr. King unveiled the latest model of the product together with other specialists.
