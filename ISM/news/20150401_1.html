Extracurricular practice – pottery

2015/04/01

The research of pottery making

On 27th march, the research group of glass including 5 members - Jianxiang Liu, Suhang Zhang, Jing Wang, Fanqing Ding, went to a pottery studio. This studio was established by a twenty two years old girl who was graduated from Ceramic Institutes of Jingdezhen. We were taught by the sister girl and different potteries such as flowerpot, brush pot and vase were created by us. Controlling the lump of argil is not easy for freshman.

3 days later, today, we went the studio again and draw some pattern on the surface of the dry argil. After this our teacher will put them into a kind of liquid to glaze them.The next step is the process of roasting the treated argil about 6 hours at 1200℃. Finally we could get our work. During this experience we know the material, process, principle and some professional knowledge about the pottery making that could help us making a better design.
