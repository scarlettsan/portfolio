The first generation of industrial design students in joint graduate school have successfully graduated

2015/03/10

On 10th March, as the first generation of graduates, 17 master students majoring in Industrial Design from SEU-Monash Joint graduate school joined their graduation thesis defense. With a excellent performance, they finally finished their studies, recognized by notable entrepreneurs, and has gotten well jobs from HUAWEI, ALIBABA , Ford and etc. Professor Xue, Ian Wong, Professor Tang and some other defense experts also attended, and gave these master students great praise.
